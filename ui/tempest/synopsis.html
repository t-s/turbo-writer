About TurboWriter

TurboWriter is a powerful, collaborative web-based authoring workbench. TurboWriter enables subject matter experts to share their knowledge with wide communities of users, and enables project teams to collaborate on an infinite variety of document types.

TurboWriter's functionality is organized into two major phases: template design, and project execution.

Template Design

Template design is the process of creating the structure of a project so that any number of projects can utilize that structure.

When you design a template, you itemize the kinds of information that will need to be written, you describe the documents that will be produced by bringing together that information, and you specify the workflow for carrying out writing assignments in a logical sequence.

In addition, you provide formatting information to enhance the appearance of the documents.

And perhaps most importantly, you create detailed guidance to the project team, imparting your expertise on how to produce each unit of content.

Once you've created a template, you can share with other TurboWriter users. That makes possible TurboWriter's second phase, project execution.

Project Execution

Project execution is the process of a team working collaboratively to write the family of documents that are to be produced by the project.

As the project owner, you begin project execution by selecting a template and giving your project a name.

Instantly, TurboWriter presents you with the action items for assigning the first group of writing assignments to members of your team. From that moment until the project is complete, TurboWriter leads you and your team members every step of the way toward producing the documents you wish to create.

In summary, TurboWriter enables subject matter experts to share their knowledge of how to carry out a particular kind of writing project, and enables project owners to execute their projects, guided by that subject matter expertise.
